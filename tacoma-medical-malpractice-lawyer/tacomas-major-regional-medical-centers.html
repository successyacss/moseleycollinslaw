What is the statute of limitations for filing a medical malpractice claim in Washington State?
In Washington State, the statute of limitations for filing a medical malpractice claim is generally three years from the date of the alleged negligent act or one year from when the injury was discovered or reasonably should have been discovered, whichever comes later. However, there are some exceptions and nuances that an experienced attorney can explain based on your specific circumstances.
