Frequently Asked Questions

What constitutes medical malpractice?
Medical malpractice occurs when a healthcare professional deviates from the standard of care, causing injury to a patient. This can include errors in diagnosis, treatment, aftercare, or health management.
How do I know if I have a valid medical malpractice claim?
To determine if you have a valid claim, you should consult a qualified medical malpractice lawyer who can evaluate factors such as the nature of the error, evidence of negligence, and resulting harm. Documentation like medical records will be crucial.
