A Seattle medical malpractice lawyer is a legal professional who specializes in representing clients in Seattle, Washington, who have been harmed due to the negligence or errors of healthcare providers. Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Seattle medical error attorney do?
A Seattle medical error attorney specializes in representing patients who have suffered harm due to medical mistakes, including misdiagnosis, surgical errors, and medication errors. They seek compensation for damages such as medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering.
How can I determine if I have a valid medical malpractice case in Seattle?
To determine if you have a valid case, consult with an experienced attorney who will review your medical records, gather expert opinions, and assess whether the standard of care was breached by a healthcare provider resulting in injury or harm.
What is the statute of limitations for filing a medical malpractice claim in Washington State?
In Washington State, the statute of limitations for filing a medical malpractice claim is generally three years from the date of the alleged malpractice or one year from when the injury was discovered or reasonably should have been discovered, whichever comes first.
How much does it cost to hire a Seattle medical error attorney?
Many Seattle medical error attorneys work on a contingency fee basis, meaning they only get paid if you win your case. The typical fee ranges between 25% to 40% of the awarded compensation. Its important to discuss fees upfront during your initial consultation.
What evidence is needed to prove a medical malpractice claim?
Key evidence includes detailed medical records, expert testimony showing that the standard of care was not met, documentation of injuries sustained as a result of the error, and any other relevant information demonstrating how negligence led to harm.
