Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifies as medical malpractice in Seattle?

Medical malpractice occurs when a healthcare professional provides substandard care that results in harm to the patient. This can include misdiagnosis, surgical errors, medication mistakes, and failure to provide appropriate treatment.

How do I find a reputable medical malpractice attorney in Seattle?

Look for attorneys who specialize in medical malpractice law, have strong client reviews, and offer free consultations. You can check local bar association directories and ask for recommendations from other legal professionals or former clients.

What is the statute of limitations for filing a medical malpractice lawsuit in Seattle?

In Washington state, you generally have three years from the date of the alleged malpractice or one year from when the injury was discovered (or reasonably should have been discovered), whichever comes later. However, there are exceptions, so its crucial to consult with an attorney promptly.
