How do you charge for your services?
We typically work on a contingency fee basis, meaning we only get paid if you win your case. Our fee is a percentage of the settlement or award, so there are no upfront costs for you.
What is the likely outcome of my case?
While I cannot guarantee specific results, based on the details youve provided and our past experiences with similar cases, we can aim for compensation that covers medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, and other related costs.
How long will it take to resolve my case?
The timeline can vary depending on the complexity of your case and whether it settles out of court or goes to trial. On average, personal injury cases can take anywhere from several months to a few years to resolve.
What information do you need from me to proceed?
Initially, well need all relevant documentation such as medical records, police reports (if applicable), any correspondence with insurance companies, witness information, and any photos or videos related to the incident. This will help us build a strong case on your behalf.
