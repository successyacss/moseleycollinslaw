Frequently Asked Questions

What experience do you have with cases similar to mine?
Its crucial to ask about the lawyers specific experience with cases like yours. A seasoned personal injury lawyer who has successfully handled similar cases will be more adept at navigating your claim.
How do you charge for your services?
Most personal injury lawyers work on a contingency fee basis, meaning they only get paid if you win your case. Typically, the fee is a percentage of your settlement or court award.
What is the expected timeline for my case?
The timeline can vary based on the complexity of the case and court schedules. Your lawyer should provide an estimated timeframe but be transparent that it could change due to unforeseen circumstances.
