Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to gather for trial preparation?
You need to gather medical records, police reports, witness statements, photos of the accident scene and damages, insurance correspondence, and any other relevant documentation.
How long does it typically take to prepare for a car accident trial?
Preparation can take several months depending on the complexity of the case. This includes gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, and possibly consulting with experts.
Will I need to testify in court?
Yes, you will likely need to testify about your recollection of the accident and how it has affected you physically and financially.
What should I wear when attending court sessions?
Dress professionally as if you were going for a job interview. Business attire is recommended—such as suits or dresses—for both men and women.
How can I help my lawyer build a strong case?
Be honest and thorough in providing all requested information promptly. Keep detailed records of medical treatments and expenses related to the accident. Follow your lawyers advice closely throughout the process.
